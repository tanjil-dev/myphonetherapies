Importance of Early Diagnosis

Learn More

Early and accurate diagnosis of emotional trauma is crucial for effective treatment and recovery. Prompt intervention can help manage symptoms and prevent long-term complications.

Psychological Evaluations for Emotional Trauma

Learn More

At MyPhoneTherapies, our team of experienced psychologists offers thorough psychological evaluations to accurately diagnose the severity and impact of your emotional trauma, including PTSD, anxiety, and depression, include:

  • Gathering Information: Collecting details about the accident, symptoms, and any previous emotional or psychological issues, including a review of medical records related to the accident.

  • Initial Interview: Conducting an in-depth conversation with the patient to understand their symptoms, history, and daily functioning.

  • Psychological Testing: Utilizing a variety of standardized tests and assessments to measure emotional and psychological impacts, using evidence-based tools and questionnaires.

  • Behavioral Observations: Observing behaviors during the evaluation to provide additional context to the findings.

  • Time Commitment: The full evaluation can take between 4 to 12 hours, depending on the complexity of your case. If the evaluation is extensive, it may be broken up into multiple sessions over two days to accommodate fatigue.

  • Feedback Session: After the psychological evaluations, there is a dedicated session where your psychologist goes over the results with you. This meeting covers the strengths, weaknesses, and any diagnoses identified. It also provides a clear interpretation of the findings, offers recommendations for further action or treatment options, and gives you a chance to ask questions and discuss any concerns about the evaluation.
