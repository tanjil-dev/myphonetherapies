Referring a Patient for Psychological Testing and Therapy is Easy

How to Refer a Patient

Referring a patient for psychological testing and therapy with MyPhoneTherapies is straightforward:

  • Complete the Online Referral Form: Complete our online referral form below.
  • Send Medical Records: Securely upload the pertinent medical records with your referral form, email to referrals@myphonetherapies.com or fax to 877-552-1745. Include all pertinent demographic information, auto insurance or premise liability insurance details with claim number and policy number, and attorney information.
  • Patient Registration: Please have the patient complete our online patient registration.
  • Contact Us for Questions: Call 888-688-6880 if you have any questions.
