Effective Counseling for Your Recovery

At MyPhoneTherapies, we offer a range of counseling services designed to support individuals recovering from auto accidents and slip and fall accident injuries.

Types of Counseling Offered:

Learn More

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): This evidence-based therapy helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors.

  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT): Focuses on teaching mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness skills.

  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR): EMDR is particularly effective for processing traumatic memories.

  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR): Utilizes mindfulness meditation to reduce stress and improve emotional well-being.

  • Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT): Specially designed to help individuals process and overcome trauma.

  • Accelerated Resolution Therapy (A.R.T.): Uses eye movements and visualizations to reprogram how traumatic memories are stored in the brain.

  • Psychoeducation: Providing information and education about mental health conditions and coping strategies.

  • Relaxation Techniques: Teaching methods such as deep breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, and guided imagery to manage anxiety and stress.

Benefits of Counseling:

Learn More

  • Personalized Treatment Plans: Our therapists create tailored treatment plans based on your unique needs and circumstances.

  • Convenient Telepsychology Sessions: Receive therapy from the comfort of your home

  • Ongoing Support: Regular follow-ups to ensure continuous progress.

  • Family Involvement: Engage your family in the counseling process for additional support.

How Telepsychology Works:

Learn More

Telepsychology allows you to connect with our therapists through secure video calls. This means you can access high-quality mental health care from the convenience of your own home. During the sessions, our therapists will guide you through various counseling methods to support your recovery.
