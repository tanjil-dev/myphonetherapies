What is EMDR Therapy?

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapy is a powerful and effective psychotherapy method used to help individuals process and recover from traumatic experiences. It is particularly beneficial for those who have experienced car accidents or slip and fall injuries, which can leave lasting emotional scars.

Why Choose EMDR for Trauma Recovery?

Learn More

EMDR therapy helps reprocess traumatic memories and reduce their emotional impact. By focusing on these memories while engaging in bilateral eye movements, patients can find relief from distress and build healthier coping mechanisms.

How Does It Work?

Learn More

Our EMDR online therapy sessions are conducted via secure video calls. During the session, you will work with a doctor or therapist who will guide you through the EMDR process, using visual stimuli or taps to facilitate bilateral stimulation. This method helps reprocess and desensitize traumatic memories, promoting emotional healing.
