Traumatic Brain Injury, Concussion, PTSD and Anxiety Testing, Treatment and Therapy for Car Accident Injuries in Delaware, Kentucky, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oregon and Washington, D.C

MyPhoneTherapies is the best choice for online psychological services in Delaware, Kentucky, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oregon and Washington, D.C. MyPhoneTherapies provides neuropsychological and psychological testing, cognitive rehabilitation, EMDR and counseling for patients who have been injured and traumatized by car accidents.

Have you been experiencing any of these conditions and symptoms:

Anxiety, fear and nervousness about driving, trouble sleeping, trouble thinking, trouble speaking or concentrating, mood swings, stress, memory loss, forgetfulness, word finding difficulty, word processing difficulty, headaches, lack of focus, difficulty in decision making, can't follow your normal routine, lack of coordination, and/or slurred speech after a car accident?

If you believe that your car accident caused any of your symptoms or if you believe that your symptoms have become worse after the car accident, and you want help, then starting is very easy.

Just complete the Patient Registration forms, once your auto insurance coverage has been verified, you will be sent a secure portal link to login, get scheduled, and begin testing, treatment and therapy all from the comfort of your home.
