Workers' Compensation Psychological and Neuropsychological Services in Wisconsin

Comprehensive Telehealth Psychological Care for Injured Workers

At MyPhoneTherapies, we specialize in providing telehealth psychological and neuropsychological services for workers injured on the job in Wisconsin. Our mission is to support your mental health and recovery, helping claimants get back to work and daily life as smoothly as possible.

Our Services

    We offer a range of specialized services tailored to meet the unique needs of workers' compensation cases:

  • Psychological and Neuropsychological Evaluations: Thorough assessments to understand the psychological and cognitive impacts of work-related injuries.
  • Cognitive Rehabilitation: Personalized programs designed to improve cognitive functions affected by injury.
  • EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing): Evidence-based therapy to process and heal from traumatic experiences.
  • Counseling: Compassionate support and guidance to help you navigate your recovery journey
