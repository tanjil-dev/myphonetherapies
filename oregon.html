Personalized Telepsychology for Traumatic Injuries in Oregon

MyPhoneTherapies provides telepsychology services to Oregon residents recovering from car accidents and slip-and-fall injuries. Telepyschology makes it easier fo your to access our doctors and therapists from your smart phone, tablet or computer.

Learn More

How It Works

  • Register Now: Click "Register Now" and complete the new patient registration forms.

  • Schedule an Appointment: Schedule an appointment, usually you can see a psychologist within 24 hours.

Seeing the Psychologist Online

  • Your First Appointment: You will have the chance to share the details of your accident and discuss your mental, emotional, and physical symptoms with the psychologist. Let them know what you have been through, in a safe and understanding environment.

  • Begin Online Tests and Assessments: Start your online tests and assessments. If you can't complete them the same day, you can log in another day to finish.

  • Receive Your Plan: Your psychologist will score your tests and create a personalized plan for you.

  • Start Counseling: Choose your therapist and begin counseling right away.

Telehealth Counseling for Traumatic Injury Recovery:

Learn More

Our licensed psychologists, therapists, and counselors provide personalized telehealth counseling to address emotional, mental and physical difficulties from traumatic injuries like car accidents and slip-and-fall accidents.

Types of counseling offered:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)

  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)

  • Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT)

  • Accelerated Resolution Therapy (A.R.T.)

  • Psychoeducation

  • Relaxation Techniques

Telepsychology Services Available Statewide in Oregon
