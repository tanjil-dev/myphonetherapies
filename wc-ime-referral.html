Comprehensive IMEs for Injured Workers

At MyPhoneTherapies, we specialize in providing IMEs for workers' compensation cases. Our team of highly qualified psychologists and neuropsychologists delivers thorough assessments to understand the psychological and cognitive impacts of work-related injuries.

Our IME Services

    MyPhoneTherapies' expert psychologists and neuropsychologists provide objective assessments with a high level of expertise, efficiency, and professionalism:

  • Psychological IMEs: Comprehensive assessments of psychological conditions resulting from work-related injuries, including depression, anxiety, and PTSD.
  • Neuropsychological IMEs: Detailed evaluations of cognitive functions affected by injuries, such as memory, attention, and executive functions.

Getting Started

To schedule an IME with MyPhoneTherapies, you have 3 choices:

  • Complete the Online IME Referral Form Below: Fill in the necessary details and upload all medical and mental health records, including raw data.
  • Email the Request: Email your IME request and all medical and mental health records, including raw data to referrals@myphonetherapies.com.
  • Fax the Request: Fax your IME request and all medical and mental health records, including raw data to 877-552-1745.
  • Please have the claimant complete the Workers Comp. Registration Form for IMEs only.

Once the complete medical and mental health records are received and reviewed, we will forward a prepay invoice to you. Once the prepayment is received, the IME date will be confirmed.

If you have any questions, please call or email us.
