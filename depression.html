Understanding Depression After an Accident

Experiencing a car accident or slip and fall injury can lead to more than just physical injuries. The trauma can also trigger emotional and psychological issues, including depression. This mental health condition is characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a lack of interest or pleasure in activities.

Why Depression Occurs:

Learn More

After an accident, the shock, pain, and disruption to daily life can lead to emotional distress. If not addressed, this distress can develop into depression. Factors contributing to depression after an accident include:

  • Physical Pain: Chronic pain from injuries can lead to feelings of helplessness and despair.
  • Loss of Independence: Injuries may require extended periods of recovery, leading to a loss of autonomy.
  • Trauma: The accident itself can be a traumatic event, causing lingering psychological effects.
