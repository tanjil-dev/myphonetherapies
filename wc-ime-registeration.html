Welcome to MyPhoneTherapies' registration page for Workers' Compensation Independent Medical Examinations (IMEs).

About the IME Process

A Psychological IME is a comprehensive evaluation conducted by a neutral, third-party medical professional to assess any psychological and neuropsychological conditions resulting from a work-related injury. The purpose of the IME is to provide an impartial evaluation of your mental health and cognitive functions.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our Medical Records Department:

  • Phone:888-688-6880
  • Or contact the third party which scheduled the IME for further information

What to Expect

  • Initial Interview: A psychologist or neuropsychologist will conduct an initial interview via telehealth to gather detailed information about your history and current condition.
  • Testing and Assessment: You may undergo various psychological and neuropsychological tests administered remotely through telehealth.
  • Reporting: Upon completion, the results of your evaluation will be forwarded to the third party that requested the IME.
