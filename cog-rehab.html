Cognitive Rehabilitation

Cognitive Rehabilitation via Telehealth for Accident Injuries with MyPhoneTherapies

What is Cognitive Rehabilitation?

Cognitive rehabilitation is a therapeutic approach aimed at improving cognitive functions such as memory, attention, problem-solving, and executive functions that have been impaired due to injury or illness.

Why Choose Telehealth for Cognitive Rehabilitation?

Learn More

Telehealth offers a convenient and accessible way to receive cognitive rehabilitation services from the comfort of your home. It eliminates the need for travel, reduces costs, and provides flexibility in scheduling.

How Does It Work?

Learn More

Our cognitive rehabilitation program utilizes advanced technology to deliver personalized therapy sessions via telehealth; you can interact with our therapists through video calls, complete cognitive exercises on specialized software, and track progress in real-time.

We Accept All Auto Insurance

Learn More
