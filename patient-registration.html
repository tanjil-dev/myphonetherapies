How to Register

Registering for psychological and neuropsychological testing and therapy with MyPhoneTherapies is simple and quick!

  • Create Your Account: Start by creating your account in the secure patient portal, powered by TherapyNotes, to schedule an appointment online.
  • Complete the New Patient Registration Forms: Please complete the New Patient Registration Forms.
  • View the Doctors' Schedule: Once logged in, you can choose which doctor of psychgology you would like to see, and choose the best time for your appointment.
  • Get Started: With all steps completed, you can an appointment quickly, all from the comfort of your home or office.
