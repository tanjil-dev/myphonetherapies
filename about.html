TELEPSYCHOLOGY

Personalized Telepsychology for Traumatic Injuries

MyPhoneTherapies offers professional telepsychology services for people recovering from car accidents, slip-and-fall injuries, and work injuries. Our expert team provides comprehensive psychological and neuropsychological evaluations to assess your specific needs. Based on these evaluations, we develop personalized treatment plans that may include cognitive rehabilitation therapy for TBI and other cognitive issues, as well as counseling protocols like A.R.T. and EMDR therapy. This comprehensive approach supports your recovery journey. Our services include:

Comprehensive Psychological and Neuropsychological Telehealth Evaluations

Learn More

At MyPhoneTherapies, we offer comprehensive psychological and neuropsychological evaluations that include standardized testing and assessments. These evaluations help identify conditions related to your injury and guide the development of personalized treatment plans.

Telehealth Counseling for Traumatic Injury Recovery

Our licensed psychologists, therapists, and counselors offer personalized telehealth counseling to address the cognitive, emotional, and mental health difficulties associated with traumatic injuries resulting from car accidents, slip-and-fall, and work injuries. We specialize in supporting individuals through their journey of managing, recovering, and healing from traumatic brain injury, anxiety, depression, pain, stress, and other mental health challenges that arise from these traumatic events.

Learn More

Various types of counseling:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)
  • Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT)
  • Accelerated Resolution Therapy (A.R.T.)
  • Psychoeducation
  • Relaxation Techniques

Cognitive Rehabilitation via Telehealth

Learn More

Our therapists provide cognitive rehabilitation therapy to help you recover cognitive functions impaired by injuries from your accident. Cognitive rehabilitation can help to improve memory, attention, and problem-solving skills, essential for returning to daily activities after the accident.
