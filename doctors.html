MyPhoneTherapies provides online video and computerized neuropsychological and psychological testing, cognitive rehabilitation, EMDR therapy, and counseling therapy for patients who have been injured and traumatized by car accidents in Delaware, Kentucky, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oregon and Washington, D.C.

Getting started is very easy.

Learn More

Just complete the Patient Registration forms, once your auto insurance coverage has been verified, you will be sent a secure portal link to login, get scheduled, and begin testing, treatment and therapy all from the comfort of your home.

MyPhoneTherapies accepts no fault, personal injury protection auto insurance for psychological and neuropsychological testing, counseling therapy, EMDR therapy and cognitive rehabilitation throughout Delaware, Kentucky, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oregon and Washington, D.C. Since Delaware, Kentucky, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oregon and Washington, D.C is a no fault state, at fault drivers may be eligible for testing, therapy and treatment.
