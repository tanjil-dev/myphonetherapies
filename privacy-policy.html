PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding our practices regarding use and disclosure of information that you may given via this site. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully so that you understand our privacy practices. By using this website, you are in agreement with the terms of this Privacy Policy and upon submitting any information via this site, you are consenting to the collection, use and disclosure of that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

This is not designed for use by persons under the age of 18. Children should always get permission from their parents before sending any information about themselves (such as their names, email addresses and phone numbers) over the Internet, to us or to anyone else. We do not knowingly allow anyone under the age of 18 to register on our site. If you are a minor, please do not give any information whatsoever without the signed consent and authorization of a legal guardian or parent. Any and all communications received from a known minor without parental authorization, including faxes and emails is destroyed and deleted by MyPhoneTherapies, LLC.

The information gathered on our website voluntarily supplied by visitors to our website when they answer specific questions or submit emails or faxes when potential patients apply to receive services from MyPhoneTherapies, LLC’s contractors is used soley to do our best to satisfy the request given by potential applicant including pursuit of payment by the insurance carrier, attorney or other parities in regard to the services. Potential applicant may be contacted in order to attempt to fulfill request by potential applicant.. Some data is gathered for marketing research purposes in order to improve website marketing quality, design and content.

To serve you better, we may combine actively collected information with personally identifiable information to communicate information to you (upon your request), for marketing and research purposes, for website personalization or for purposes as outlined in this Privacy Policy. We will take reasonable measures to prevent personally identifiable information from being combined with passively collected information, unless you consent otherwise.

    We may disclose your personally identifiable information to our partners in the US and internationally, upon their agreement to treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Additionally, we may disclose your personally identifiable information to third parties, located in the United States and/or internationally, specifically:

  1. To contractors we use to support our business (e.g., fulfillment services, technical support, delivery services, and financial institutions); in these cases we will require third parties to agree to treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy;
  2. In connection with the sale, assignment, or other transfer of the business of this site to which the information relates; in these cases we will require any such buyer to agree to treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy; or
  3. Where required by applicable laws, subpoena, court orders, or government regulations.

In addition, we will make full use of all information acquired through this site that is not in personally identifiable form.

To keep your personally identifiable information current and accurate, please contact us as specified below. We will take reasonable steps to update or correct personally identifiable information in our possession that you have previously submitted via this site.
